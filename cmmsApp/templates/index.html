How we work

GSE Project Workflow

A seamless journey from initial inquiry and technical FAT quality checks to global delivery and dedicated aftersales support

  1. 1

    Inquiry & Operational Requirements

    We analyze your constraints to ensure the product meets your specific throughput and safety needs.

  2. 2

    Product Selection & Planning

    We define the technical specifications and fleet standardization requirements, assessing diesel vs. electric (eGSE) feasibility for the specific unit.

  3. 3

    Manufacturing & FAT Quality Checks

    After production we conduct Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) to verify performance and QA alignment before the product leaves the factory.

  4. 4

    Supply Coordination & Delivery

    Our team manages final configuration, documentation, and logistics to ensure the product is delivered seamlessly to your facility.

  5. 5

    Rollout & Aftersales Support

    We provide on-site commissioning, training coordination, and spares planning to ensure long-term product reliability.
